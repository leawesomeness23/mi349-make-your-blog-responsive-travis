About Me

Hello! I'm Drew Travis, a passionate coder, gamer, and game developer who loves to share projects.

Outside of coding, I am passionate about racing, sports, and anything to do with cars. I plan to apply my coding and design expertise to some kind of racing application later in the future.

My main interests came from my childhood, growing up for some reason I always found myself playing or watching videogames, and this shaped me in ways that are almost inexplicable. I played a lot of racing and car related games as a kid, as well as sports and other themed games, but the most common type was racing. This led me to become obsessed with cars as well as motorsport, pretty much defining who I am today.
